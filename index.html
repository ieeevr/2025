About the IEEE VR Conference

IEEE VR, the premier international conference and exhibition on virtual reality (VR) and 3D user interfaces, stands as a beacon of innovation and collaboration in the realm of immersive technologies. Renowned for its groundbreaking research presentations, hands-on workshops, and cutting-edge demonstrations, IEEE VR brings together a diverse community of researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts from around the globe. Attendees have the opportunity to delve into the latest advancements in VR technology, explore emerging trends, and engage in stimulating discussions on the future of immersive experiences. With its commitment to fostering interdisciplinary dialogue and pushing the boundaries of VR and 3D user interfaces, IEEE VR continues to inspire and shape the future of virtual reality innovation.

About Saint-Malo, Brittany France

Situated on the rugged coast of Brittany in northwestern France, Saint-Malo enchants visitors with its rich history, captivating architecture, and picturesque landscapes. Enclosed within formidable stone walls, this fortified port city exudes an old-world charm, offering a glimpse into its maritime past. Wander through the narrow cobblestone streets of the intra-muros (walled city) and discover charming cafes, boutiques, and historic landmarks such as the imposing Saint-Malo Cathedral. With its bustling harbor, Saint-Malo has long been a hub of maritime activity, evident in its bustling fish market and maritime museum. Beyond the city walls, visitors can explore stunning beaches, including the renowned Plage du Sillon, or embark on a journey to the nearby Mont Saint-Michel, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Whether strolling along the ramparts, indulging in fresh seafood, or simply soaking in the maritime ambiance, Saint-Malo offers a captivating blend of history, culture, and natural beauty that leaves a lasting impression on all who visit.
