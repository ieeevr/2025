Paper Video Presentation Guidelines

While the presentations at IEEE VR 2025 will take place in person, we are committed to ensuring the conference's accessibility offering an online experience for attendees. To this end, plenary sessions will be streamed live, and paper presentations videos will be made available on the conference's on-line channels. Below, you will find guidelines for creating the video.

  • Duration: The videos should have a duration of 10 minutes, corresponding to the regular presentation time.
  • Template: Use the IEEE VR 2025 template for your video, especially for the title page. The template can be found here: IEEE VR 2025 Slide Templates.
  • Resolution and Format: The video must be 1080p and in MP4 format.
  • Subtitles: Subtitles are mandatory and must be included in your video.
  • File Size: The video file size must not exceed 250MB.
  • Optional Content: You may include photos or videos of the authors, though this is not mandatory.
  • Copyright Compliance: Since videos will be posted and accessible on-line later, ensure that all content used is royalty-free.

Uploading Your Video

Providing your video is mandatory. To ensure a smooth process, please upload your video to PCS by February 21, 2025, AoE.

Thank you for your collaboration in making IEEE VR 2025 an accessible and engaging conference. We deeply appreciate your contributions.
